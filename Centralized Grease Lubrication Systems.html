Manual Grease Pump

Manual grease pump meant for feeding grease to bearing & other moving part of machines & other lightly machines having 1 to 20 lubrication point. These pump is made up of purely transparent acrylic reservoir for storage of grease, for sensing the Grease level a tell tale rod is provide and follower plate assembly for help in positive suction of grease. It contains two outlet part of M10xx1 BSP right and left side in which one is used as air bleed valve & second port used as o outlet. To check reverse flow of grease, a non return valve is provide in the system at the outlet port.
Grease can be filled manually or with another pump. Filling from pump. M10x1 BSP/grease nipple port is provide. Manually, we have to remove follower plate, spring,top cover and fill the grease from the top of the reservoir and to fit all the components by pressure. For air removing unscrew the bleed plug pull the handle maximum out and press the follower plate by knob till grease comes out from bleed plug.

Multiline Radial Lubricator

Multiline radial lubricator is best suited for machine/plant where lubrication is required with high pressure continuously / intermittently for individual point. This lubricator are used for machine / plant like rolling mill, sugar plant, cement plant, rubber mixing machine, forging hammer, stone crusher, stone iron plant etc.

This lubricator comprise of single pumping station having a number of plunger pump placed radically and fitted on to the outer periphery of the housing. Each plunger unit is given a reciprocating motion with the help of a cam. The cam rotates with the help of an electric motor and speed reduction gear box. The dose per plunger pump / cycle is 0.05 to 0.25cc (adjustable) with maximum pressure 200 Kg/cm². These all plunger pump are connected to the lubrication point directly / progressive block by mean of tube, rubber hose, compression type fitting, clamping etc.

Grease Filling in the reservoir can be done manually as well as with the help of other pump. For pump ¼" BSP port is provided on reservoir.

  1. Always use proper & Clean lubricants. Avoid foreign particles in the pump. The foreign particles or dust items are the main responsible factor to damage physical components of the pump and chocking in the plunger body.
  2. Always fill the lubricants before it is empty to avoid air in the pump. The Presence of air may be resulted in improper quantity of discharge.
  3. Should always careful with regular maintenance of plungers and reservoir for its long life.
  4. Do not plug out put of any pumping unit.

Progressive Blocks

Progressive distributor blocks of HPB-4555 (Secondary Line) /HPB-6080 (Primary Line) series are meant for distribution of grease / oil delivered by a pump. From this progressive block we get lubricant in sequently one after another. Any one outlet is blocked then the progressive block will stop functioning. with suitable monitoring devices, this blockages is sensed and suitable warning is given to avoid running the machine without proper lubrication. These blocks can be used for intermittent / continuous lubrication.

A progressive distributor block can be used in a number of industries, including steel plants, paper plants, heavy-duty power presses, printing machines, textile machines, packaging machines, metal-working machines, material handling equipment, injection molding machines, mobile equipment, chassis lubrication, and so on.

Hand Operated Bucket Grease Pump

A hand-operated bucket grease pump is an essential tool for maintaining the smooth operation of heavy machinery and equipment. Grease pumps are designed to lubricate moving parts, such as bearings, gears, and joints, preventing wear and tear and extending the lifespan of the equipment.

A hand-operated bucket grease pump is a cost-effective and practical solution for those who need to lubricate their equipment without the use of electricity or compressed air. These pumps are typically made from high-quality materials such as steel or aluminum, which makes them sturdy and durable. They also have a compact design, making them easy to transport and store.

Using a hand-operated bucket grease pump is simple and easy. The pump is filled with grease and attached to the equipment that needs lubrication.

Air Operated Bucket Grease Pump

An air-operated bucket grease pump is an efficient and reliable tool used for lubricating heavy machinery and equipment. These pumps are designed to deliver high-pressure lubrication, ensuring that the equipment's moving parts remain lubricated and functional.

Air-operated bucket grease pumps use compressed air to operate, making them more powerful and efficient than their manual counterparts. They are typically made from durable materials such as steel or aluminum, which can withstand the high pressures and demands of industrial applications.

One of the main advantages of using an air-operated bucket grease pump is its ability to provide a continuous flow of grease.
